About Me

Hi, I'm Tyson Ngo, but most people know me as "TenZ." I'm a professional esports player from Canada, specializing in Valorant. I was born on May 5, 2001, and ever since I can remember, I've had a passion for first-person shooters. Known for my precise aim and aggressive, innovative playstyle, I quickly caught the attention of fans and competitors.

Achievements

VCT Masters Reykjavik 2021

In May 2021, Sentinels emerged as the champions of the Valorant Champions Tour (VCT) Masters Reykjavik, the first international LAN event for Valorant. They achieved an undefeated run, showcasing dominant performances throughout the tournament. TenZ played a pivotal role, with his exceptional aim and innovative strategies helping secure their historic victory, marking Sentinels as the first global champions in Valorant history.

AVL 2023

Sentinels won the AfreecaTV Valorant League (AVL) 2023 with an impressive and undefeated run. They kicked off their campaign by defeating Paper Rex 2-1, followed by a 2-0 victory over DRX, and a hard-fought 2-1 win against Team Liquid. In the grand final, Sentinels secured a decisive 3-0 victory over Paper Rex, demonstrating their dominance and strategic prowess throughout the tournament.

VCT Masters Madrid 2024

The Sentinels clinched the VCT Masters Madrid 2024 title with a thrilling 3-2 victory over Gen.G Esports. The grand finals saw a fierce battle, with Gen.G taking early leads by winning on Breeze and Ascent, but Sentinels staged a comeback with victories on Bind, Split, and Icebox.
